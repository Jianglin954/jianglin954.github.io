Biography

Jianglin Lu received his M.E. degree from the College of Computer Sciences and Software Engineering, Shenzhen University, in July 2022. His research interests include machine learning, deep learning, and computer vision, optimization, and data mining.

He spent a wonderful time working with his supervisor, Jie Zhou, at Shenzhen University, and his supervisor, Alexander Lapin, at Kazan Federal University.
