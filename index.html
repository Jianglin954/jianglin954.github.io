Biography

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the SMILE Lab of the Department of ECE, Northeastern University (NEU), under the supervision of Prof. Yun Raymond Fu. I have interned at Amazon, Adobe Research and Tencent Music Entertainment. My current research interests primarily focus on large language models, vision-language models, multimodal alignment, reinforcement learning, and agents.
I am looking for a part/full time internship position in Spring/Summer 2026. Please feel free to contact me if you have any openings!
