Biography

Jianglin Lu received his M.E. degree from the College of Computer Sciences and Software Engineering, Shenzhen University, in July 2022. His research interests include machine learning, deep learning, and computer vision, optimization, and data mining. Specficially, he has a board reasearch interest in graph neural network, learning to hash, multimodal retrieval, learning with noisy labels, transfer learning, and visual-language pretraining, etc.

He was so lucky to work with Jie Zhou, Alexander Lapin, and Witold Pedrycz.
